Instagram Non-Followers Checker

Find who isn't following you back (Public profiles only)

How to use:

  1. Go to your Instagram profile in a web browser
  2. Click on Followers and wait for all to load
  3. Option 1: Press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select all, then Ctrl+C/Cmd+C to copy, and paste below
  4. Option 2: Save as text file (Ctrl+S/Cmd+S) and upload below
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 for Following
  6. Click "Compare" to see results
